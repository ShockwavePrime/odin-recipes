Beef Ribs

Description

Slow cooker ribs recipe with homeade BBQ sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1(6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 pounds beef back ribs

Steps

  1. Combine water, ketchup, tomato paste, brown sugar, vinegar, mustard, and salt in a slow cooker; stir well to dissolve brown sugar and salt.
  2. Place short ribs into the sauce and stir to coat.
  3. Cover and cook until ribs are tender, about 8 hours on Low or 4 hours on High.
