Pizza

Description

Easy to make pizza on grill that gives a classic smoky, word fired oven flavor.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomatoes
  • Black Olives
  • Roasted red Peppers
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil
  • (Opt.)Toppings

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Make the garlic oil, then brush onto the grill grates
  3. Shape the dough and place it on hot grill
  4. Flip the crust and brush with oil
  5. Top the pizza however you like, close the lid, and cook until cheese melts
