Guacamole

Description

Avocado based dip from Mexico, get that fresh kick of cayenne and cilantro. Enjoy with some chips to the side.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Vegetables
  • Herbs and Spices

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl
  2. Mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper
  4. Serve or refrigerate
